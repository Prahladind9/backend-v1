Dynamic Programming
    is a method for solving OptimizationProblems by
    breaking a problem into smaller sub-problems.

    OptimizationProblem
    > Enumeration,                ex: climb n steps
    > Minimization/Maximization,  ex: Stock buy/sell, over-days to get profit
    > Yes/No Questions,           ex: form 10$ with x pennies, z quarters?

Problems
1) Stairs Climbing
2) Cutting Stick/Rod
